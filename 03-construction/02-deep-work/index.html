    Deep Work

    The ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task.

    How do I enter and sustain states of deep concentration?

    In short: Deep work is not a mystical flow state. It is a skill built through ritual, environmental design, and a deep understanding of my own ignition patterns.

    The Core Insight

    For the INTP 5w4 ASD‑1 mind, deep work is both natural (hyperfocus on interesting problems) and fragile (easily shattered by interruption). The key is not to force focus but to design conditions that protect it. This module builds a complete deep work system tailored to my neurology.
